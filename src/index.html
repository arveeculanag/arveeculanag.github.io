Projects

I have developed several projects throughout my entire career as a developer, and below are some of my best to date.

Guess That Compound

An android quiz game application that lets the users guess what the name of the compound that is display on the screen with a given time constraint. Can you guess the compound? Furthermore, it has a learn feature where users can read "byte" or "small" size lessons to learn something everyday.

Musical Programming

The combination of two of my favorite passions, music and programming comes alive with this project. Play musical notes using hand gestures and a camera, each hand gesture is assigned a musical note.

Logger

A web application that lets you keep track of your activities both online and offline. There is also a feature where the web app automatically creates charts for you to better visualize your activites such as the time you spent during that particular activity, what medium you used for that, and more. The best part is that its totally free for everyone to use and redistribute.
